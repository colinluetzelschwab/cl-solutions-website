## Design Context

### Users
Swiss SMB owners (bakeries, clinics, law firms, studios, tradespeople) who need a professional website but don't want to deal with agencies that take months and charge hourly. They're browsing on their phone during a break or on desktop after work. They want to see: this studio is legit, fast, and reasonably priced. They're comparing against Wix/Squarespace DIY and traditional agencies.

### Brand Personality
**Confident. Precise. Approachable.**
Not cold corporate. Not startup-bro. A craftsman who takes pride in their work — think Swiss watchmaker precision meets a friendly conversation over coffee. Colin is a one-man operation using AI automation, but the client-facing brand should feel like a boutique studio.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Refined minimalism with warmth. NOT the typical "dark mode with neon accents" AI aesthetic.
- **Theme**: Dark mode — the audience is business owners browsing after hours, and the Three.js water shader hero demands a dark canvas.
- **References**: Linear.app (precision), Vercel (confidence), Rauno Freiberg's personal site (craft), Stripe (approachability)
- **Anti-references**: Generic agency templates with stock photos. Anything with gradients on everything. Neon-on-black "hacker" vibes. Overly playful/startup-y aesthetics.
- **Colors**: Move away from pure blue (#4169FF). Consider a warm neutral palette with a single distinctive accent — perhaps a refined amber/gold, a muted sage, or a warm coral. The accent should feel intentional, not decorative.
- **Typography**: Geist is locked (brand consistency). Sharp corners (0 radius) is locked.
- **Key differentiator**: The Three.js water caustic shader in the hero — this is the memorable moment. Everything else should support it, not compete.

### Design Principles
1. **Substance over style** — Every element earns its place. No decoration for decoration's sake.
2. **Quiet confidence** — The design should whisper "we're good" not shout "LOOK AT US."
3. **Swiss precision** — Tight spacing system, consistent rhythm, nothing misaligned.
4. **Warmth in restraint** — Dark doesn't mean cold. Warm tints in the neutrals, approachable copy.
5. **One memorable moment** — The shader hero is the wow. Everything else is composed restraint.
